What This Podcast Is

Two Cents is a bi-weekly horror podcast hosted by people who love the genre, hate bad decisions, and have no interest in pretending every movie is good. Each month revolves around a theme, because chaos is better when it’s organized.

Theme-based months Full spoilers, always Sarcasm encouraged Hot takes included
